Our Mission

Bridging Culture, Building Community.
We strive to preserve the richness of Vietnamese heritage while empowering students through leadership, cultural identity, and a lasting sense of belonging within the Mercer family.

Chào Mừng! (Welcome)

The Vietnamese Student Association (VSA) at Mercer University is more than just a club. It's a home away from home. Whether you are of Vietnamese descent or simply a fan of the culture (and the food!), we provide a space for all students to connect and grow.

From our annual cultural showcases to casual social hangouts, Mercer VSA is dedicated to fostering cultural awareness and community engagement on campus. Join us as we celebrate our roots and create new traditions together.
